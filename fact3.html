languages

Zimbabwe is the country with the most offical languages in the world.
Currently zimbabwe has 16 different languages and won the Guinness
World Recod in 2013 as the counrty with the most official languages at
a national level. The languages spoken include Chewa, Chibarwe, English,
Kalanga, Koisan, Nambya, Ndua, Ndebele, Shangani, Shona, sign language,
Sotho, Tonga, Tswana, Venda and Zhosa.
